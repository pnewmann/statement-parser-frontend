Do Not Sell My Personal Information

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Statement Scan does NOT sell your personal information.

We have never sold personal information, and we have no plans to do so. Your financial data and personal information are not for sale.

Our Commitment

Statement Scan, Inc. ("Statement Scan," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. We want to be completely transparent about our data practices:

  • We do NOT sell personal information to any third party for any purpose
  • We do NOT share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • We do NOT use advertising networks or ad-tracking technologies
  • We do NOT monetize your data in any way

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of their personal information.

Since Statement Scan does not sell or share personal information as defined under the CCPA/CPRA, there is no sale or sharing to opt out of. However, we honor this right and provide this page for transparency.

What We DO With Your Data

We only use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing our service: Processing your portfolio data and generating analytics
  • Account management: Managing your account if you create one
  • Customer support: Responding to your inquiries
  • Service improvement: Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to improve our product
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our legal obligations

Local Processing: Most data processing happens directly in your browser. Your uploaded statements are parsed locally and never sent to our servers unless you explicitly save a portfolio to your account.

Third-Party Service Providers

We work with carefully selected service providers who help us operate our service. These providers:

  • Are contractually bound to protect your data
  • May only use data to provide services to us
  • Cannot use your data for their own purposes
  • Cannot sell or share your data with others

Our service providers include cloud hosting, authentication services, and Plaid (for brokerage connections). None of these arrangements constitute a "sale" under applicable privacy laws.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request information about the personal data we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Verification

When you submit a privacy request, we may need to verify your identity to protect your data. This typically involves confirming the email address associated with your account.

Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf. The agent must provide written authorization, and we may require you to verify your identity directly with us.

Response Time

We respond to verifiable privacy requests within 45 days. If we need more time (up to an additional 45 days), we will notify you of the reason and extension period.

Changes to This Notice

We may update this notice from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
