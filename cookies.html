Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Statement Scan, Inc. ("Statement Scan," "we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at statementscan.app.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

We also use similar technologies, including:

  • Local Storage: Stores data in your browser with no expiration date
  • Session Storage: Stores data for the duration of your browser session
  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images used to track user behavior

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security features (CSRF protection)
  • Load balancing and server routing

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a better experience:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Redaction toggle setting
  • Announcement bar dismissal
  • Last selected options and sort preferences

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics to understand how visitors use our service. This helps us improve the user experience. Analytics data is anonymized and does not include any personally identifiable financial information.

What We Do NOT Use: Statement Scan does not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, third-party ad network cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any form of cross-site tracking. We do not serve ads and do not sell your data to advertisers.

Specific Cookies We Set

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration
session_token User authentication Strictly Necessary Session / 30 days
csrf_token Security (prevents cross-site request forgery) Strictly Necessary Session
theme_preference Remembers dark/light mode choice Functional 1 year
announcement_dismissed Remembers dismissed announcements Functional 30 days
redaction_preference Remembers PII redaction toggle Functional 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Plaid: When you connect a brokerage account, Plaid's authentication flow may set cookies. These are governed by Plaid's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to display typography. Google may collect anonymized usage data.

Local Storage Usage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to:

  • Store non-sensitive user preferences
  • Cache UI state for better performance
  • Remember form inputs during your session

Important: Portfolio data that you process locally is NOT stored in cookies or local storage. Your financial documents exist only in memory during your active session and are discarded when you close the page, unless you explicitly save a portfolio to your account.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Set preferences for certain types of cookies

Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent Statement Scan from functioning properly. You may not be able to sign in or use certain features.

For more information about cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting. Statement Scan respects DNT signals. When we detect a DNT signal, we disable any non-essential analytics tracking.
